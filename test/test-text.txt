The ancient tower stood silent beneath the silver moon. Kaelen looked up, his cloak fluttering in the cold wind. He knew that the artifact he sought lay somewhere within those crumbling walls.

"Are you sure about this?" Elara whispered, her hand resting on the hilt of her sword. Her eyes reflected the pale moonlight, filled with a mixture of fear and determination.

Kaelen nodded slowly. "The prophecy was clear. If we don't find the Star of Eldoria tonight, the shadows will consume the kingdom by dawn. We have no other choice."

They approached the massive stone doors. Runes carved into the surface began to glow with a faint, sapphire light. The air hummed with ancient energy, making the hair on Kaelen's arms stand up.

Suddenly, a low growl echoed from the darkness. Something was guarding the entrance. Something that hadn't seen the sun in a thousand years.

"Get ready," Kaelen said, drawing his staff. The crystal at its tip sparked with golden fire. "We aren't alone."
